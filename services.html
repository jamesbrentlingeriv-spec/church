What to Expect

Contemporary Worship

Energetic, Spirit-filled music that honors God and prepares hearts for the message.

At our core, we are a people of the Presence. Our worship is marked by apostolic fervor and an unashamed devotion to the King of Kings. We invite the Holy Spirit to have His way, using energetic melodies and Spirit-anointed harmonies to usher in the glory of God. This is a time of preparation—where the noise of the world is silenced and the fire of the Spirit is kindled—preparing a highway for the Lord to speak directly into the lives of His people through the preached Word.

Welcome to our Family

You're Welcome Here!

Kingdom Journey is a real church for real people, and we want to help you become the person God made you to be.

No matter where you are in your journey, you're invited to discover your purpose and live it out with others here at Kingdom Journey.

You'll find a casual, laid-back environment with friendly people, great praise and worship and down-to-earth teaching where you can grow your faith, discover your purpose and find community. Our desire is for all of us to become the people God created us to be. We believe he will do amazing things in and through us! We look forward to seeing you at Kingdom Journey.

Kingdom Kids in Divine Service

Kingdom KIDS is more than a Sunday program; it is a partnership. Our devoted team of educators joins forces with parents to guide children—from birth through 6th grade—on a journey toward becoming fully devoted followers of Christ. By blending age-specific biblical truths with practical, real-life applications, we ensure that every child leaves equipped for the week ahead.

We believe the Bible is the most captivating story ever told. Through immersive activities, dynamic video teaching, and engaging discussions, we bring Scripture to life in a way that sticks. At Kingdom KIDS, we foster a high-energy environment where faith meets fun, helping every child develop a lifelong love for God's Word.

Fellowship Time

Connect with others over coffee and refreshments before and after services.

Fellowship at our house is an intentional extension of the altar—a time where the joy of the Lord spills out from the sanctuary and into our conversations. We believe that spiritual growth happens in circles, not just rows. Our fellowship time is a high-energy environment designed for building authentic relationships, sharing testimonies of God's goodness, and strengthening the bonds of our church family. Whether you are a long-time member or a first-time guest, this is your opportunity to connect deeply, laugh loudly, and experience the warmth of a KINGDOM united in Christ.
