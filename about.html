About Us

Discover our story, our mission, and what we believe

Our Story

Kingdom's Journey began with a vision to create a place where people from all walks of life could encounter the life-changing power of Jesus Christ. Founded in Nicholasville, Kentucky, our church has grown from a small gathering of believers to a vibrant community of faith.

We are a Pentecostal church that believes in the power of the Holy Spirit, the authority of God's Word, and the importance of authentic community. Our desire is to see lives transformed by the Gospel and to equip believers to fulfill their God-given purpose.

Whether you're taking your first steps in faith or looking for a church home, we welcome you to join us on this journey of growing deeper in Christ and reaching wider with His love.

Our Mission

To glorify God by making disciples who love God, love people, and transform communities through the power of the Holy Spirit. We exist to help people know Jesus, grow in faith, and go serve others.

Our Vision

To be a thriving community of believers who are passionate about worship, committed to prayer, grounded in Scripture, and engaged in serving our local community and beyond. We envision multiplying disciples and establishing kingdom influence throughout Kentucky.

What We Believe

The Bible

We believe the Bible is the inspired Word of God and the final authority for faith and practice.

Jesus Christ

We believe in the deity of Jesus Christ, His virgin birth, sinless life, miracles, and sacrificial death.

The Holy Spirit

We believe in the baptism of the Holy Spirit with evidence of speaking in tongues and gifts of the Spirit.

Salvation

We believe salvation comes through repentance and faith in Jesus Christ, resulting in eternal life.

The Church

We believe in the spiritual unity of believers in Christ, called to worship, fellowship, and service.

Second Coming

We believe in the imminent return of Jesus Christ and the resurrection of both saved and lost.
